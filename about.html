About Us

3140 Active Wear was created as a student-led ecommerce project to provide affordable, high-quality activewear for everyday athletes. We believe in community, inclusivity, and delivering performance gear that doesn’t break the bank.

Our Mission

To empower individuals of all fitness levels by providing affordable, durable, and stylish activewear while fostering a strong community of support and motivation.

Our Vision

To become a trusted name in activewear for students and community members, while serving as a showcase of what can be achieved through collaborative student projects.

Our Values

Community

We believe in building a supportive network for our customers and peers.

Sustainability

We strive to source materials and processes that are eco-friendly.

Quality

We deliver products that meet high standards for comfort, performance, and style.
